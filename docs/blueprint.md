# **App Name**: CuraObesa

## Core Features:

- Product Presentation: Display detailed information about the '7 Rituais da Cura para Acabar com a Obesidade' product, including benefits and testimonials.
- Purchase Option: Enable direct purchase of the '7 Rituais da Cura para Acabar com a Obesidade' product through a secure payment gateway.
- Customer Support Chat: Implement a live chat feature for instant customer support and queries related to the product.
- Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ): Address common questions and concerns about the product and its effectiveness.
- Success Stories AI Tool: Use AI to dynamically select and showcase relevant customer success stories or testimonials based on user demographics and interests. The AI will incorporate testimonials that highlight specific benefits a given user might find most relevant.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#008080), chosen for its calming and trustworthy association with health and wellness.
- Background color: Very light teal (#E0F8F8), providing a soft and clean backdrop that complements the primary color without overwhelming it.
- Accent color: Muted gold (#B8860B), used to highlight key information and calls to action, conveying value and importance.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif font, giving an elegant, intellectual and contemporary feel.
- Use clean, modern icons that represent different aspects of health and wellness, like nutrition, exercise, and mental health.
- Ensure a responsive design that adapts seamlessly to various devices (desktops, tablets, and smartphones), with a focus on mobile-first optimization.
- Incorporate subtle animations and transitions to create a smooth and engaging user experience.
